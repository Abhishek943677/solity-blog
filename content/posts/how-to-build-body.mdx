If you're a 20-year-old looking to get started with bodybuilding, here are some tips to keep in mind:

<!--EndFragment-->

1. Start with a solid nutrition plan: Eating a balanced diet that is high in protein is essential for muscle growth. Aim to consume at least one gram of protein per pound of body weight each day. You'll also need to eat plenty of complex carbohydrates and healthy fats to fuel your workouts and support muscle recovery
2. Follow a structured workout plan: It's important to have a plan in place that focuses on compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses, which work multiple muscle groups simultaneously. Make sure to also incorporate isolation exercises to target specific muscle groups. You should aim to work out at least three to four times per week.

4. Lift heavy weights: To build muscle, you need to challenge your body with progressively heavier weights. Start with a weight that you can comfortably lift for 8-10 reps and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
